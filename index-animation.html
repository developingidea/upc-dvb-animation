    Take the remote control of your satellite receiver and switch the device on
    by press the red button on the top left of the remote control.
    Then, press “menu” button of the remote.
    On TV screen, select the following items with the arrows and validate by pressing the OK button of the remote control.
    Select “set-up”, press OK. Then “signal settings”, press OK Then “Channels Auto-search”, press OK.
    Enter your PIN Code. By default “1234” or “0000”. If the code is wrong, please call our customer service before the last attempt and request a refresh of the smart card.
    Our customer service agent will provide your further instructions.
    The scan starts. Press OK when it is completed.
    You can always refer to the manual of the satellite receiver.
